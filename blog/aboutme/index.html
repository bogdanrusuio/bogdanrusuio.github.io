About the Author

The tasks I solve at work provide value to a company which in turn compensates me. That compensation enables me to do the things I’m passionate or curious about. I enjoy writing about and sharing the things that interest me the most. My interests vary widely; I’ll narrow in on the things I’m most interested in.
